Qualtrics@Harvard

Qualtrics is web-based survey software available for use by Harvard faculty, students and staff to support teaching and research at Harvard. Surveys can be created and distributed by anyone with a Harvard PIN login. Ideal for:

  • Simple one-time surveys to ongoing research
  • Targeting respondents by e-mail address
  • Publicly available surveys
  • A wide range of question types
  • Branching and piping
  • Robust reporting
